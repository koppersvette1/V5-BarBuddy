BarBuddy V5 - Palate Calibration
Philosophy: "Good" is subjective. Use this guide to diagnose what a guest actually wants based on what they say.
I. TRANSLATING GUEST REQUESTS
| Guest Says... | They Probably Want... | Avoid... | Recommend |
| "Not too sweet" | Balance (Sour/Bitter), not zero sugar. | Syrupy Tiki drinks. | Margarita, Collins, Gold Rush. |
| "Strong" | Spirit-Forward (booze taste), not just high ABV. | Fruity/Juicy drinks. | Old Fashioned, Martini, Negroni. |
| "Refreshing" | Citrus, Carbonation, Ice. | Creamy/Heavy drinks. | Mojito, Mule, Gin Fizz. |
| "Fruity" | Berry/Tropical flavors, masking alcohol. | Bitter/Herbal drinks. | Cosmo, French 75, Clover Club. |
| "Smooth" | Low burn, sweet finish. | Rye, High-Proof, Dry. | Bourbon Old Fashioned, Amaretto Sour. |
II. THE FLAVOR STAR
Use this to balance your own creations. Opposites balance each other.
Sweet balances Sour and Bitter.
Sour balances Sweet and Heat (Spice).
Bitter balances Sweet.
Salt enhances Sweet and suppresses Bitter.
