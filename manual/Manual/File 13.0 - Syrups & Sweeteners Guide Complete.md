File 13.0 - Syrups & Sweeteners Guide Complete
BarBuddy V5 - Sugar Science
Philosophy: Sugar is a flavor carrier, not just a sweetener. Making your own syrups is the cheapest and most impactful upgrade you can make to your home bar.
I. THE CORE ESSENTIALS (Tier 1)
1. SIMPLE SYRUP (1:1)
Ratio: 1 cup White Sugar : 1 cup Water.
Method: Combine in a jar and shake until dissolved (no heat needed) or heat gently.
Use: Daiquiri, Collins, Gin Sour, Mojito.
Profile: Neutral sweetness. Allows spirits to shine.
2. RICH DEMERARA SYRUP (2:1)
Ratio: 2 cups Demerara/Turbinado Sugar : 1 cup Water.
Method: Must boil on stove to dissolve fully.
Use: Old Fashioned, Tiki, Coffee Cocktails.
Profile: Caramel, molasses, heavy texture (mouthcoating).
3. HONEY SYRUP (3:1)
Ratio: 3 parts Honey : 1 part Hot Water.
Why: Pure honey freezes into a lump when it hits ice. Adding water makes it pourable.
Use: Bee's Knees, Gold Rush, Penicillin.
Profile: Floral, earthy.
II. FRUIT SYRUPS (Tier 2)
4. GRENADINE (Real Pomegranate)
Ingredients: 1 cup Pomegranate juice (POM Wonderful) + 1 cup Sugar + 1 tsp Orange Flower Water.
Method: Heat juice and sugar until dissolved. Add flower water off heat.
Use: Jack Rose, Tequila Sunrise, Shirley Temple.
Profile: Tart, fruity, tannic. DO NOT buy Rose's (red corn syrup).
5. RASPBERRY / STRAWBERRY SYRUP
Method (Maceration):
Toss 1 cup fresh berries with 1 cup sugar in a bowl.
Let sit for 2-4 hours (sugar pulls juice out).
Mash and strain out solids.
Use: Clover Club, Bramble, flavored Margaritas.
Profile: Bright, fresh berry flavor (better than boiling).
6. PASSION FRUIT SYRUP
Method: 1 cup Passion Fruit Pulp (frozen is fine) + 1 cup Sugar. Heat to dissolve.
Use: Hurricane, Zombie, Pornstar Martini.
Profile: Tropical, tart, exotic.
III. SPICE & HOLIDAY SYRUPS (Tier 3)
7. CINNAMON BARK SYRUP
Method: Simmer 1 cup water + 1 cup sugar + 3 crushed cinnamon sticks for 10 mins. Let steep for 1 hour.
Use: Zombie, Spiced Old Fashioned, Fall drinks.
Profile: Warm, spicy, "Red Hots" candy note.
8. VANILLA BEAN SYRUP
Method: Split 1 vanilla bean (scrape seeds). Simmer with 1 cup water + 1 cup sugar.
Hack: Add 1 tsp Vanilla Extract to 1 cup Simple Syrup (cheaper/faster).
Use: Pornstar Martini, Espresso Martini, Cream Sodas.
Profile: Creamy, dessert-like.
9. GINGER SYRUP
Method: Blend fresh ginger root (peeled) + Simple Syrup in blender. Strain through cheesecloth.
Use: Penicillin, Moscow Mule.
Profile: Spicy "burn" that mimics alcohol bite.
10. CARDAMOM SYRUP
Method: Simmer 1 cup water + 1 cup sugar + 10 crushed cardamom pods. Steep 1 hour.
Use: Gin cocktails, Coffee drinks.
Profile: Floral, spicy, exotic.
11. HOLIDAY SPICE SYRUP (Allspice/Clove)
Method: Simmer 1 cup water + 1 cup sugar + 1 cinnamon stick + 4 cloves + 1 star anise + orange peel.
Use: Hot Toddies, Winter Old Fashioneds.
Profile: Christmas in a glass.
IV. SPECIALTY & ADVANCED (Tier 4)
12. ORGEAT (Almond)
Ingredients: Almonds, sugar, orange flower water.
Use: Mai Tai, Japanese Cocktail.
Profile: Nutty, floral, creamy.
Buying: Hard to make well. Recommended to buy (Liber & Co).
13. COFFEE SYRUP
Method: 1 cup strong brewed coffee + 1 cup sugar. OR leftover cold brew + sugar.
Use: Espresso Martini, White Russian, Breakfast Stout.
Profile: Roasted, bitter-sweet.
14. OLEO SACCHARUM ("Oil Sugar")
Method:
Peel 3 lemons (peels only).
Bury peels in 1 cup sugar in a bowl.
Let sit 4 hours. The oil will liquefy the sugar.
Use: Punches, High-end lemonades.
Profile: Intense, pure citrus oil flavor (no juice acidity).
15. TEA SYRUP (Earl Grey/Chai)
Method: Brew 1 cup VERY strong tea (4 bags). Dissolve 1 cup sugar into it while hot.
Use: Tea-based sours, Old Fashioneds.
Profile: Tannic, floral, complex.
V. SUGAR SUBSTITUTES & STORAGE
SUGAR-FREE OPTIONS
Agave Nectar: Use straight. Good for Tequila. Vegetal note.
Maple Syrup: Use straight. Good for Bourbon. Smoky note.
Monkfruit/Stevia: Dissolve granular sweetener 1:1 with water.
Warning: Lacks "body" (viscosity). Add a pinch of Xanthan Gum to thicken if drink feels watery.
STORAGE & SHELF LIFE
Simple Syrup (1:1): 1 month (Refrigerated).
Rich Syrup (2:1): 3-6 months (Refrigerated).
Fruit Syrups: 2 weeks (Refrigerated) - Add 1 oz Vodka to extend life.
Signs of spoilage: Cloudiness or mold spots. When in doubt, toss it out.
