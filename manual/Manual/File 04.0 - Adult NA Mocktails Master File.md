PART 2: THE 25 RECIPES (DETAILED)
I. SPIRIT-STYLE (Using NA Alternatives)
RECIPE #1: SEEDLIP MARTINI
Category: Spirit-Style NA
Base: Seedlip Grove 42 (Citrus)
Standard Spec:
2.5 oz Seedlip Grove 42
0.5 oz Verjus (White)
Lemon Twist for garnish
Instructions: Stir well with ice, strain into frozen glass.
Why It Works: Verjus provides the acidity and tannin structure of Dry Vermouth without alcohol.
RECIPE #2: RITUAL WHISKEY SOUR
Category: Spirit-Style NA
Base: Ritual Zero Proof Whiskey
Standard Spec:
2 oz Ritual Whiskey Alternative
0.75 oz Lemon Juice
0.75 oz Simple Syrup
1 Egg White (or 1 oz Aquafaba)
Instructions: Dry shake (no ice), wet shake (ice), strain.
Why It Works: Egg white provides heavy mouthfeel to replace the body of alcohol.
RECIPE #3: LYRE'S NEGRONI
Category: Spirit-Style NA
Base: Lyre's Spirits
Standard Spec:
1 oz Lyre’s Dry London Spirit
1 oz Lyre’s Italian Orange
1 oz Lyre’s Aperitif Rosso
Instructions: Stir with ice, strain over large cube, express orange peel.
Why It Works: Bitterness lingers on the tongue just like real Campari.
RECIPE #4: THE "G&T" (GARDEN & TONIC)
Category: Spirit-Style NA
Base: Seedlip Garden 108
Standard Spec:
2 oz Seedlip Garden 108
4 oz Elderflower Tonic
Cucumber Ribbon
Instructions: Build in glass over ice.
Why It Works: Savory pea/hay notes pair perfectly with quinine.
RECIPE #5: NA OLD FASHIONED
Category: Spirit-Style NA
Base: Spiritless Kentucky 74
Standard Spec:
2 oz Spiritless Kentucky 74
0.25 oz Demerara Syrup
3 dashes NA Aromatic Bitters
Instructions: Stir with large ice cube.
Why It Works: Heavy syrup adds viscosity to the thinner NA spirit.
II. TEA-BASED (Tannins = Complexity)
RECIPE #6: EARL GREY SOUR
Category: Tea-Based
Base: Earl Grey Tea
Standard Spec:
2 oz Strong Earl Grey Tea (chilled)
0.75 oz Lemon Juice
0.75 oz Vanilla Syrup
1 Egg White
Instructions: Dry shake, wet shake, strain.
Why It Works: Tea tannins dry out the mouth like alcohol.
RECIPE #7: HIBISCUS MARGARITA
Category: Tea-Based
Base: Hibiscus Tea
Standard Spec:
2 oz Strong Hibiscus Tea
1 oz Lime Juice
0.5 oz Agave Nectar
Salt Rim
Instructions: Shake with ice, strain over fresh ice.
Why It Works: Hibiscus is naturally tart and tannic, mimicking tequila's bite.
RECIPE #8: OOLONG OLD FASHIONED
Category: Tea-Based
Base: Roasted Oolong Tea
Standard Spec:
2 oz Roasted Oolong Tea (chilled)
0.25 oz Maple Syrup
2 dashes NA Bitters
Instructions: Stir over ice.
Why It Works: Roasted tea has smoky/woody notes similar to scotch.
RECIPE #9: MATCHA COLADA
Category: Tea-Based
Base: Matcha
Standard Spec:
1 tsp Matcha Powder
3 oz Pineapple Juice
1 oz Coconut Cream
0.5 oz Lime
Instructions: Shake vigorously to dissolve matcha.
Why It Works: Grassy matcha cuts through the pineapple sugar.
RECIPE #10: CHAI SPICED MULE
Category: Tea-Based
Base: Masala Chai
Standard Spec:
2 oz Chai Concentrate
0.5 oz Lime Juice
4 oz Ginger Beer
Instructions: Build in copper mug.
Why It Works: Double spice hit from ginger beer and chai spices.
III. SHRUB-BASED (Vinegar = Burn)
RECIPE #11: STRAWBERRY BALSAMIC FIZZ
Category: Shrub-Based
Base: Strawberry Shrub
Standard Spec:
1.5 oz Strawberry Vinegar Shrub
0.5 oz Balsamic Glaze
4 oz Soda Water
Black Pepper garnish
Instructions: Build over ice, stir gently.
Why It Works: Vinegar provides a "throat burn" similar to alcohol.
RECIPE #12: APPLE CIDER SHRUB MULE
Category: Shrub-Based
Base: Apple Cider Vinegar
Standard Spec:
1 oz Apple Cider Vinegar (Raw)
0.5 oz Maple Syrup
4 oz Ginger Beer
Instructions: Build in mug.
Why It Works: ACV has a fermented funk that mimics cider/brandy.
RECIPE #13: PINEAPPLE JALAPEÑO SMASH
Category: Shrub-Based
Base: Pineapple Shrub
Standard Spec:
1.5 oz Pineapple Shrub
2 slices Jalapeño (muddled)
0.5 oz Lime
Soda Water
Instructions: Muddle jalapeño, shake with shrub/lime, top with soda.
Why It Works: Capsaicin heat releases endorphins like alcohol.
RECIPE #14: BEET & BERRY
Category: Shrub-Based
Base: Beet Juice
Standard Spec:
1 oz Beet Juice
0.5 oz Raspberry Vinegar
0.5 oz Simple Syrup
2 oz Tonic Water
Instructions: Stir and strain into coupe, top with tonic.
Why It Works: Earthy flavors feel sophisticated, not like soda.
RECIPE #15: PEACH BASIL SMASH
Category: Shrub-Based
Base: Peach Shrub
Standard Spec:
1.5 oz Peach Shrub
4 Basil Leaves
Soda Water
Instructions: Muddle basil, shake with shrub, double strain, top with soda.
Why It Works: Basil adds peppery anise notes.
IV. BOTANICAL & SAVORY
RECIPE #16: CUCUMBER COLLINS
Category: Botanical
Base: Cucumber Water
Standard Spec:
4 Cucumber slices (muddled)
0.75 oz Elderflower Syrup
0.75 oz Lemon
Soda Water
Instructions: Muddle, shake, strain over fresh ice, top with soda.
RECIPE #17: ROSEMARY GRAPEFRUIT SPRITZ
Category: Botanical
Base: Grapefruit Juice
Standard Spec:
2 oz Grapefruit Juice
0.5 oz Rosemary Syrup
3 oz Tonic Water
Torched Rosemary sprig
Instructions: Build in wine glass. Torched herb mimics gin botanicals.
RECIPE #18: THE NO-JITO
Category: Botanical
Base: Mint
Standard Spec:
10 Mint Leaves
1 oz Lime
0.75 oz Syrup
Soda Water
Instructions: Gently muddle mint, build with crushed ice.
RECIPE #19: SPICY TOMATO (VIRGIN MARY)
Category: Savory
Base: Tomato Juice
Standard Spec:
4 oz Tomato Juice
0.5 oz Lemon
Worcestershire, Tabasco, Pepper
Celery Salt Rim
Instructions: Roll back and forth (don't shake).
RECIPE #20: SALTED WATERMELON COOLER
Category: Savory
Base: Watermelon Juice
Standard Spec:
3 oz Watermelon Juice
0.5 oz Lime
Pinch Sea Salt
Instructions: Shake and strain. Salt makes the fruit pop.
V. DESSERT STYLE
RECIPE #21: COFFEE TONIC
Category: Dessert
Base: Cold Brew
Standard Spec:
4 oz Tonic Water
2 oz Cold Brew Concentrate
Orange Peel
Instructions: Pour tonic first, float coffee on top.
RECIPE #22: ESPRESSO MARTI-NO
Category: Dessert
Base: Espresso
Standard Spec:
2 oz Espresso
0.5 oz Vanilla Syrup
1 oz Heavy Cream
Instructions: Shake HARD with ice for foam.
RECIPE #23: GOLDEN MILK PUNCH
Category: Dessert
Base: Oat Milk
Standard Spec:
3 oz Oat Milk
0.5 oz Turmeric Ginger Syrup
Nutmeg Garnish
Instructions: Shake and strain.
RECIPE #24: CHOCOLATE ORANGE OLD FASHIONED
Category: Dessert
Base: Black Tea
Standard Spec:
2 oz Black Tea
0.5 oz Chocolate Syrup
2 dashes NA Orange Bitters
Instructions: Stir over ice.
RECIPE #25: LAVENDER FIZZ
Category: Dessert
Base: Lemon
Standard Spec:
1 oz Lavender Syrup
1 oz Lemon
1 Egg White
Soda Water
Instructions: Dry shake, wet shake, top with soda (soufflé style).
