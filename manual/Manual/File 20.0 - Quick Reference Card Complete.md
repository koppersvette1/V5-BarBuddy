BarBuddy V5 - The Cheat Sheet
Print this page and tape it behind your bar.
STANDARD RATIOS
Sour: 2 Spirit : 1 Sweet : 1 Sour
Old Fashioned: 2 Spirit : 0.25 Sweet : 2-3 Dashes Bitters
Martini/Manhattan: 2 Spirit : 1 Vermouth
Highball: 2 Spirit : 4-6 Mixer
Negroni: 1 Spirit : 1 Bitter : 1 Sweet Vermouth
SMOKE GUIDE
Times:
Mesquite / Hickory: 4-6 Seconds (Strong)
Oak / Cherry: 8-10 Seconds (Medium)
Apple / Pecan: 10-12 Seconds (Mild)
Pairing by Spirit:
Whiskey: Oak, Cherry, Hickory
Tequila: Mesquite, Citrus woods
Gin: Apple, Pear (Light fruit only)
Rum: Cherry, Oak, Maple
Vodka: Peach, Apple (Very light)
MEASUREMENTS & CONVERSIONS
1 oz = 30 ml
1 barspoon = 1 tsp (5 ml)
1 dash = ~1/8 tsp (roughly)
1 egg white = ~1 oz
1 lemon = ~1 oz juice
1 lime = ~0.75 - 1 oz juice
SHELF LIFE CHEATSHEET
Citrus Juice: 24 Hours (Oxidizes fast)
Simple Syrup: 3-4 Weeks (Refrigerated)
Rich Syrup (2:1): 3-6 Months (Refrigerated)
Vermouth: 1-2 Months (MUST Refrigerate)
Cream Liqueurs: 1 Year (Refrigerated)
Spirits/Bitters: Indefinite
SUBSTITUTIONS
Simple Syrup: Maple Syrup, Agave, Honey (diluted).
Cointreau: Grand Marnier, Triple Sec, Curacao.
Lemon/Lime: Vinegar (in tiny amounts), Citric Acid solution. NEVER bottled juice.
Egg White: Aquafaba (1 oz chickpea water).
MOCKTAIL & KID RULES
Mocktails: No alcohol, no bitters (unless NA), complex flavors allowed.
Kid-Friendly: No alcohol, NO caffeine, NO spice (unless requested), visually fun (colors/foam).
